# HUMEA Lab

Human Measurement and Analysis (HUMEA) is an open laboratory environment, where facilities are located in one open space. In addition to lab facilities data collection and analysis methods are essential in HUMEA. Methods for field measurements outside lab spaces are being developed.

Laboratory is developed in close collaboration with Sports and Exercise Medicine researchers at Institute of Biomedicine.

Facilities of the laboratory include:

[Motion Lab and Physiology Lab](https://sites.uef.fi/humea/humea-laboratory/human-motion-and-performance-analysis/)

[Robot-assisted rehabilitation development environment](https://sites.uef.fi/humea/humea-laboratory/robot-assisted-rehabilitation/)

[Driving simulator environment](https://sites.uef.fi/humea/humea-laboratory/driving-simulator/)
